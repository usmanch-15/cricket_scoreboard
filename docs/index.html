Privacy Policy — Cricket Scoreboard

Effective date: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cricket Scoreboard ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. This app is developed by Usman Ch.

What we collect

  • Match data you enter: team names, player names, scores, and ball-by-ball events. This is stored in our database (Supabase) to let you resume and review your matches.
  • An anonymous device identifier, automatically generated, used only to keep your matches separate from other devices. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

How we use it

Match data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: saving, resuming, and reviewing your cricket matches. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising, and we do not use it for any purpose beyond running the app.

Data storage and security

Data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure with access restricted so that each device can only read or modify its own matches. You can permanently delete any match at any time from the History screen.

Third parties

We use Supabase (https://supabase.com) as our backend database provider. No other third-party services receive your data. We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
