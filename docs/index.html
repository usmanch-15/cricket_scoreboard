Privacy Policy — Cricket Scoreboard

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Cricket Scoreboard ("the app") is developed by Usman Ch.

What we collect

  • Match data you enter: team names, player names, scores, and ball-by-ball events. This is stored in our database (Supabase) to let you resume and review your matches.
  • An anonymous device identifier, automatically generated, used only to keep your matches separate from other devices. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personal information.

How we use it

Match data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: saving, resuming, and reviewing your cricket matches. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising.

Data storage

Data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. You can delete any match at any time from the History screen, which permanently removes it from our database.

Third parties

We use Supabase (https://supabase.com) as our backend provider. No other third-party services receive your data.
